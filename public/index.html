Steps to Obtain Stream URL and Passkey

  1. Sign in to the YouTube account from which you wish to stream.
  2. Click on your profile icon located at the top right corner.
  3. Select "Go Live" from the dropdown menu.
  4. Within the live streaming interface, you'll find your unique stream URL and passkey. Simply copy and paste them here.
  5. Once you've copied the details, you're all set to go live! Just click "Submit" to finalize.
